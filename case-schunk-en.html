Context

At Schunk Xycarb Technology (SXT) heb ik gedurende 6 months gewerkt aan het versnellen en verbeteren van de operatie by translating machine and planning data om te zetten naar stuurinformatie en concrete verbeteringen.

SXT is a manufacturing company where machine efficiency, planning and customer communication cruciaal zijn voor het succes van de operatie.

Challenge

There were three major challenges:

  • Limited transparency: How efficiently machines ran daily and where bottlenecks occurred was unclear
  • Staff Planning: Staff planning did not align well with available workplaces/machines, leading to moments of over- or understaffing
  • Unreliable SAP processing times: Processing times in SAP were often incorrect, making planning and customer communication unnecessarily difficult

Assignment

Create insight based on data into machine performance and bottlenecks, Ã©n dit vertalen naar verbeteringen that make planning more reliable and can be monitored structurally over time.

Approach / What I Did

1. Value Stream Mapping

Set up Value Stream Map (VSM) om flow, wachttijden en knelpunten in het proces zichtbaar te maken. This provided an initial overview of where time was lost.

2. Collect and Clean Data

Collected and cleaned machine and process data (SAP/Excel) (SAP/Excel) en vertaald naar eenduidige definities/KPI's. Without this step, reliable analysis was impossible.

3. Power BI Dashboard

Developed Power BI dashboard dat dagelijks automatisch laat zien:

  • How efficiently machines have worked
  • Where bottlenecks were and how long they lasted
  • Trend development so improvements are continuously monitored

4. Capacity vs. staffing analysis

Capaciteit vs. bezetting geanalyseerd: vergelijking tussen beschikbare werkplekken/machines en personeelsplanning. This clearly showed where the mismatch was.

5. Improve SAP Processing Times

Developed new working method for SAP processing times zodat planning gebaseerd wordt op realistische tijden i.p.v. historische/onjuiste waarden.

Methodology

The approach combined Lean Manufacturing principles (VSM, bottleneck analysis) with modern analytics tools (Power BI) and ERP optimization (SAP). This ensured improvements weren't just visible, but also structurally embedded and continuously monitored.
