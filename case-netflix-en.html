Context

For Netflix, I managed a Stranger Things S5 screening combined with a Clubhouse experience in Amsterdam. The event consisted of multiple components with strict time slots: arrival, hospitality, preview screening, and then guest flow into the Clubhouse activities.

Challenge

Operations had to run extremely tight due to limited time per group and high requirements for experience and security. Within 30 minutes, 250 guests had to:

  • enter
  • drop off coats (cloakroom)
  • receive food/drinks
  • be seated in the correct theatre on time for the preview

Afterwards, the same group had 15 minutes to exit and move on to the next activities, while the venue was immediately reset for the next group.
